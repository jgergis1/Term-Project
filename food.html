Best Area for Food

Zamalek, Cairo Egypt

Zamalek is a an upscale neighbohood in Cairo, Egypt. Often times reffered to as the "Manhattan of Egypt", Zamalek holds some of the finest resturants in Egypt. I not only say this as a resident myself, but as a certified foodie. Zamalek sits on a small island in between the Nile with beautiful foliage, and walkable green spaces. There are plenty of places to immerse yourself into authentic Egyptian cusine. I would suggest vising Abou El Sid to try some Koshari, Falafel, or an assortment of mezze. Beyond just traditional Arabic food however, you can find an array of different cusines. Zamalek is a popular town amongst expats and young people, the food reflects the energy of Egypt's growing food scene.

Resturant Worth Visting

Royal Maxim Palace Kempinski Cairo - Bab al Qasr

In my opinion, the best resturnat in Cairo, and my go-to recommendation for anyone travelling is the beautiful Bab al Qars (directly translates to "door of the palace") in the Royal Maxim Palace Kempinski Cairo. The vibe is immaculate, while you enjoy live entertaintment be sure to sip on some of their homemade mint lemonade. The view of the Nile is perfect to enjoy, especially in the evening as you feel the coastal winds. Many of the hotels in Egypt offer adequate dining options, but in my opinion both the location and ambiance of Bab al Qars makes it a worthwile trip.
